Hot chocolate

Hot chocolate is the perfect solution for a snowy day.

Hot chocolate is one of the most delicious hot drinks ever,

perfect for enjoying in the winter.

Making it at home is simple, and if you are looking for a recipe for a creamy,

velvety hot chocolate then you are in the right place!

Ingredients

  • Whole milk
  • Dark chocolate
  • Cornstarch
  • Sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. finely chop the dark chocolate
  2. dissolve it in a double boiler
  3. add milk, cornstarch and cocoa powder in a small pan
  4. place on the stove and heat it up
  5. add the sugar and keep stirring till it boils
  6. add the melted chocolate and stir it
  7. pour into mags
